Mode² brings intelligent data collection and actionable insight to every factory. Mode² elevates Overall Equipment Effectiveness from the machine level to the system level. Isolate system bottlenecks, identify machine failure modes, and track downtime on a case-by-case basis. Remain informed at all times. Gain real-time access to customizable alerts. Analyze historic reports and view live metrics from desktop and mobile devices. Live factory views allow all personnel to know the state of the plant floor and where employees are currently working. Beautiful dashboards provide a live heads-up display of your factory floor. Understand employee productivity through machine logging and live updates. Simply put, Mode² provides an all-encompassing view of the modern factory.
